## How he answers when pressed

- **When pressed for a specific tactic**: pivots to a portfolio-company example. Almost every theoretical claim has a specific founder/company attached.
- **When asked for personal vulnerability**: leans in (Knowledge Project on family scarcity, Quest pod on therapy/mask), but reframes the personal story as a generalizable founder lesson within seconds.
- **When asked about controversy**: deflects to constructive narrative ("here's what we built"), sometimes with a sharper edge. On the "die slow" tweet: hired crisis PR, apologized, tied it to broader narrative ("can't pull your punch").
- **When pressed on a thesis weakness**: openly acknowledges, then reframes constraints. On Posterous: openly recounts the failures (CEO ambiguity, Instagram, architecture). Unusual willingness to publish his own pitch-meeting failure stories.
