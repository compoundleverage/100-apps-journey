- Writes in headline-driven listicle structure on Medium ("5 Steps", "3 Things", "Should You Be the CEO?")
- Uses concrete portfolio companies as evidence in nearly every post
- Voice register is mentor-to-founder; rarely academic, never corporate
- Direct address ("you", "we", "us") dominates — almost no third-person abstract
- Pop-culture references (Katamari, jazz, gaming) used as load-bearing metaphors, not decoration
