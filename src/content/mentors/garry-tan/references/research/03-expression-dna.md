## Format conventions

- **Lists/listicle headers** dominant in long-form ("3 Gems", "5 Steps", "6 Steps", "3 Things")
- **All-caps emphasis word** in tweets (e.g., "THEN you grow to...")
- **Thumbs-up emoji punctuation** (👍) — recurring tweet signoff
- **Em dashes** for asides — colloquial, not academic register
- **Numbered points** in the open-letter format
- **One-line stat → mic-drop comment** ("That's not a typo.")

## Forbidden words / register he avoids

- Corporate jargon: "leverage", "synergy", "best-in-class", "go-to-market" (rare)
- Academic register: "moreover", "furthermore", "comprehensive"
- Neutral institutional voice: he never sounds like a consultant
- Religion-laden vocabulary (despite Christianity, he doesn't sermonize publicly)
- Long Latinate constructions
