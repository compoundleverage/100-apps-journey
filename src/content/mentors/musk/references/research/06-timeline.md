# Agent 6 — Timeline (Musk)

## Pre-1995: Origins

- **1971-06-28** — Born Pretoria, South Africa, to Errol Musk (engineer) and Maye Haldeman Musk.
- **~1980** — Parents divorce. Lives primarily with father (later described as abusive). Reads compulsively (Asimov, Adams, Heinlein).
- **1983** — Programs and sells "Blastar" video game to magazine for $500.
- **1989** — Moves to Canada (mother's citizenship). Studies at Queen's University.
- **1992-1995** — Transfers to Wharton (UPenn). Earns BA Physics + BA Economics.
- **1995** — Briefly enrolls at Stanford PhD physics program; drops out after 2 days to start internet venture.

## 1995-2004: Internet wealth creation

- **1995** — Founds Zip2 with brother Kimbal. Online business directories for newspapers.
- **1999** — Compaq acquires Zip2 for ~$307M. Musk personally clears ~$22M.
- **1999** — Founds X.com, online financial services.
- **2000** — X.com merges with Confinity (Peter Thiel, Max Levchin). Becomes PayPal.
- **2000-09** — Musk ousted as CEO during honeymoon (Thiel/Levchin take over).
- **2002-07** — eBay acquires PayPal for $1.5B; Musk receives $165M pre-tax.

## 2002-2010: Foundational bets

- **2002-03** — Founds SpaceX with $100M personal capital. Mission: multi-planetary species.
- **2003** — Tesla Motors founded by Eberhard, Tarpenning. Musk joins as chairman + lead investor (Series A) February 2004.
- **2006** — "Master Plan, Just Between You and Me" published.
- **2006-08-04** — Tesla Roadster prototype unveiled.
- **2006** — SolarCity founded by cousins Lyndon and Peter Rive; Musk is chairman.
- **2008** — Falcon 1 fourth flight reaches orbit (after three failures). Tesla Roadster begins customer deliveries. Musk becomes Tesla CEO October 2008. Personal financial near-collapse averted by NASA COTS contract (Dec 2008).
- **2010** — Tesla IPO June 29, 2010.

## 2010-2018: Scale and validation

- **2012** — SpaceX Dragon docks with ISS — first private spacecraft.
- **2012** — Tesla Model S launched.
- **2014-06-12** — "All Our Patent Are Belong To You" Tesla blog post.
- **2014-10** — At MIT, Musk warns AI is "summoning the demon."
- **2015** — OpenAI co-founded by Musk, Altman, Brockman, Sutskever, Hoffman, others as nonprofit AI safety lab.
- **2016-07** — Master Plan Part Deux published.
- **2016** — Solar City acquired by Tesla (later contested in shareholder lawsuit).
- **2016** — Founded Neuralink (brain-computer interfaces) and The Boring Company.
- **2017-09-29** — IAC Australia keynote: "Making Humans Multi-Planetary."
- **2018** — Tesla Model 3 production hell. Musk sleeps on factory floor.
- **2018-02-05** — SpaceX Falcon Heavy debut launch. "Starman" Tesla Roadster sent toward Mars orbit.
- **2018-08-07** — "Funding secured" tweet → SEC settlement (Sept 2018), Musk steps down as Tesla chairman, $20M fine.

## 2019-2022: Hardening, expansion, conflict

- **2019** — Resigned from OpenAI board (citing conflict with Tesla AI work). OpenAI begins commercial pivot.
- **2020-05** — SpaceX Crew Dragon Demo-2: first US crewed orbital launch since 2011 (with Boeing's Starliner still delayed years later).
- **2020-07** — Tesla becomes most-valuable carmaker by market cap, surpassing Toyota.
- **2020-2021** — Tesla profitability sustained; Model Y launches. Tesla joins S&P 500.
- **2021** — SpaceX Inspiration4 (first all-civilian orbital crew). Time Person of the Year.
- **2021-12** — Babylon Bee interview signals political pivot.
- **2022-04-14** — Twitter acquisition offer ($44B).
- **2022-07-08** — Musk attempts to terminate the Twitter deal.
- **2022-10-27** — Twitter acquisition closes.
- **2022-11** — Mass layoffs at Twitter. "Extremely hardcore" memo. Reinstates suspended accounts including Trump.

## 2023: AI race + X transformation

- **2023-03-01** — Master Plan Part 3 announced at Tesla Investor Day.
- **2023-04** — Twitter renamed X.
- **2023-04** — Founds xAI as separate AI company.
- **2023-09** — Walter Isaacson biography published.
- **2023-11** — Grok-1 released.

## 2024: Political crystallization + product pushes

- **2024-01-30** — Tornetta v. Musk Delaware ruling: 2018 Tesla compensation package voided. Musk threatens to move Tesla incorporation to Texas (later does).
- **2024-03-01** — Files lawsuit against OpenAI (later withdrawn, then refiled).
- **2024-08** — Grok-2 released.
- **2024-07** — Endorses Trump after assassination attempt; ramps up donations.
- **2024-10-10** — "We, Robot" event: Robotaxi (Cybercab) and Robovan unveiled. Optimus humanoid demo (some operations were partly remote-controlled, per later reporting).
- **2024-11** — Trump wins election. Musk is heavily aligned, frequent at Mar-a-Lago.
- **2024-11-12** — Trump announces Department of Government Efficiency (DOGE) co-led by Musk and Vivek Ramaswamy.

## Last 12 months (2024-Q2 to 2025-Q2): Highest priority for current persona

- **2024-12 to 2025-01** — Musk active in cabinet formation, public spats with Trump aides over H-1B policy (Musk pro, MAGA base anti).
- **2025-01-20** — Trump inauguration. Musk attends prominently. DOGE formally begins operations.
- **2025-02** — Grok-3 released. Claims "smarter than any other AI."
- **2025-02 to 2025-04** — DOGE makes large cuts to federal workforce. Severe public backlash; protests at Tesla showrooms; Tesla brand damage measurable in surveys + sales.
- **2025-03** — xAI acquires X in all-stock transaction (~$80B X for ~$33B equity = effective merger of the two).
- **2025-03 to 2025-05** — Reportedly increasing tension between Musk and Trump aides; Musk's position in DOGE more constrained as agenda items conflict (e.g., USPS, FAA which oversee SpaceX, EV credits which fund Tesla).
- **2025-04** — Public statements about pulling back from DOGE responsibilities to focus on Tesla.
- **2025-05** — Tesla Q1 earnings disappointing; brand damage from political alignment cited as factor by analysts. Musk publicly states he will reduce DOGE involvement.

## Near-term trajectory signals

1. **Tesla brand damage** is testing whether the political alignment was worth it. Q1 2025 sales declines are evidence; whether this corrects depends on whether DOGE is wound down quickly or continues to drag.
2. **Optimus and Robotaxi timelines** continue to slip. Musk's public framing remains aggressive (2025 production at scale); engineering reality lags.
3. **xAI / Grok competitive position** vs OpenAI / Anthropic / Google is being established. Compute commitments (Memphis Colossus cluster of 100k+ H100s) suggest Musk is willing to outspend competitors.
4. **AI safety positioning** has effectively been abandoned in his public framing; replaced with "truth-seeking AI" rhetoric. This is a major persona shift from 2014-2018.
5. **Personal-political alignment** is the most volatile variable. Musk-Trump relationship dynamics through 2025 are still resolving.

## Notes on staleness

- This persona will be most stale on near-term political dynamics (Musk-Trump fallout, DOGE wind-down) and product timelines (Robotaxi launch, Optimus production). The values, mental models, and expression patterns are stable across at least 5 years and likely longer.
- Re-research recommended quarterly for political/business dynamics.
