### Trajectory observations

1. **Output rhythm is steady**: ~1-2 major essays/talks per year + 4-6 viral tweets per quarter. The 2025-26 window is unusually rich relative to 2018-2021 (Tesla locked-down years).

2. **Pedagogy → philosophy transition**: 2022-2024 was "rebuild everything from scratch" (Zero to Hero, micrograd, nanoGPT). 2025-2026 added "name the patterns" (Software 3.0, ghosts/animals, decade of agents, vibe coding). He's consolidating from teacher-of-implementation to philosopher-of-paradigm.

3. **Diplomatic re-engagement with Tesla**: tested FSD 13.x publicly in 2025 ("amazed"). Suggests bridges intact.

4. **Increasingly hedged on AGI timelines**: 2018-2022 was bullish; 2024-2026 is "decade", "still hard", "march of nines", "today's models produce slop." Calibration tightening.

5. **Eureka Labs status (May 2026)**: LLM101n in progress; not yet shipped at scale. Course materials in development. Funding/team status not publicly detailed.
