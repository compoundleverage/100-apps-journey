## 4. Tesla vision-only (rejecting LiDAR) — 2018-2022

[score: P=3 V=3 A=3 C=3 total=12]
URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hx7BXih7zx8 (CVPR 2021 talk on vision-only)

**Stated reasoning:**
- "When radar and vision disagree, which one do you believe? Vision has much more precision, so better to double down on vision than do sensor fusion."
- Cars are designed for human eyes; the road system is built for vision-based agents.
- LiDAR is "a crutch" — extra sensors introduce complexity, cost, and failure modes without proportionate gain.
- Building "an animal from the ground up" — biological vision as architectural reference.

**Public critiques:**
- Many AV experts disagreed (Waymo, Mobileye took multi-sensor route)
- Tesla's removal of ultrasonic sensors (post-Karpathy) drew customer criticism for degraded behavior

**What this reveals about his mental model:**
- First-principles "what does this actually need" lens
- Comfortable being contrarian when the math is clear
- Bet on data scale + vision-only beating multi-sensor pipelines

**Where his stated principles diverged from action:**
- He has acknowledged in 2025 that self-driving is "not solved" — diplomatic walk-back from earlier confident timelines.
